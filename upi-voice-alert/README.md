# 💰 UPI Voice Alert

Automatically speaks out loud when you receive money via UPI apps.

## Supported Apps
- Google Pay
- PhonePe  
- Paytm
- BHIM UPI
- Amazon Pay
- WhatsApp Pay

---

## 📲 HOW TO GET THE APK (Step by Step - No Coding Needed!)

### Step 1: Create a FREE GitHub Account
1. Go to **https://github.com**
2. Click **Sign up** (top right)
3. Enter your email, create a password, choose a username
4. Verify your email

### Step 2: Create a New Repository
1. After logging in, click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Repository name: `upi-voice-alert`
3. Make sure **Public** is selected
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3: Upload All Files
1. On your new empty repository page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag and drop ALL the files from this ZIP folder
   - ⚠️ Important: Upload files maintaining the folder structure
   - Or use GitHub Desktop app (easier for folders)
3. Scroll down, click **Commit changes**

### Step 4: Watch it Build (takes ~3-5 minutes)
1. Click the **Actions** tab on your repository
2. You'll see "Build APK" running (yellow circle = in progress)
3. Wait for it to turn green ✅

### Step 5: Download Your APK
1. Click on the completed **Build APK** job
2. Scroll to the bottom to find **Artifacts**
3. Click **UPI-Voice-Alert-APK** to download
4. You'll get a ZIP file — unzip it to get `app-debug.apk`

### Step 6: Install on Your Phone
1. Send the APK to your phone (WhatsApp, email, Google Drive, USB)
2. On your phone, go to **Settings → Security → Unknown Sources** → Enable
   - Or when you open the APK, it'll ask you to allow installation
3. Tap Install

---

## 📱 First Time Setup on Your Phone
1. Open **UPI Voice Alert** app
2. Tap **"Grant Notification Permission"**
3. Find **UPI Voice Alert** in the list → Toggle it **ON** → Allow
4. Come back to the app — status should show ✅ Active
5. Tap **"Test Voice"** to hear it working!

## ❓ Troubleshooting
**App not speaking?**
- Make sure notification permission is ON (Settings → Notification Access)
- Check your phone volume is not muted
- Try the Test button to verify voice works

**Not detecting my UPI app?**
- The app covers all major UPI apps
- Make sure your UPI app shows notifications (check its notification settings)
